Job Title: Machine Learning Engineer

We are looking for a Machine Learning Engineer to join our AI team. The ideal candidate
will have strong experience in Python, machine learning, and deep learning frameworks
such as TensorFlow or PyTorch. Responsibilities include building and deploying NLP and
computer vision models, working with large datasets, and collaborating with cross-functional
teams to bring AI products to production.

Requirements:
- Bachelor's or Master's degree in Computer Science, Data Science, or related field
- 2+ years of experience with Python and machine learning libraries (scikit-learn, TensorFlow, PyTorch)
- Experience with natural language processing (NLP) and text embeddings
- Familiarity with cloud platforms (AWS, GCP, or Azure)
- Strong understanding of data structures, algorithms, and software engineering practices
- Experience with SQL and data pipelines is a plus
- Excellent communication and problem-solving skills
